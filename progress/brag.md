### Photobook Android

Performance: Speed up photos processing

The image picker in the photobook app was very slow. This was because before creating a project we have to select images . For every single image we had to check its image dimension if it were correct or not. and also check its orginal image type as we were only enabling jpegs and pngs. It took around three to four seconds to load an album with around 500 images.
I used Kotlin coroutines to take utilization of the IO threading to reduce the timing by 1/4th. Furthermore, I made improvements to the checking of the dimension and that of the image types by suing the same file descriptor which reduce the time by half. Finally I made one additional imrpovemnt by not loading 500 images at a time rather 150 images where by the remaining will be loaded in batches when the user scrolls down. All of these improvement lead to photo picker to be loaded in around 150 milliseconds.
